The project Graphic Design Sampling is about the way to disprove the precondition of design ; be FRESH and UNIQUE. However, design, not only design all the thoughts, cannot be originated itself, rather all those are derived from others. If we cannot be totally free from influences, start from being completely derivative. I began by applying the statistical sampling as the design method to interpret the machinery graphic design world, and then created the sort of blueprint, consists of the frame of graphic design works, by borrowing the concept of musical sampling. The result is the collection of all existing works.
